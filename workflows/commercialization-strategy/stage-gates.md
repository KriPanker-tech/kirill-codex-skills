# Commercialization Strategy Stage Gates

## Portfolio Registry Gate

Reviewer mode: `portfolio-review`

Approve only if:

- all identifiable projects are listed or exclusions are explained
- missing data is marked as `data insufficient`
- facts and hypotheses are separated
- project type, stage, business model, effect horizon, and risks are captured where possible
- the output is usable for strategic gap diagnostics

## Strategic Gap Gate

Reviewer mode: `gap-review`

Approve only if:

- the strategic gap is supported by portfolio evidence or marked as a hypothesis
- effect timing and portfolio maturity are discussed
- R&D logic and commercial logic are separated
- small-tonnage chemistry is framed as a testable commercial contour, not a guaranteed answer
- western practices are used as benchmarks, not fake proof

## Strategic Directions Gate

Reviewer mode: `directions-review`

Approve only if:

- directions are concrete management lanes
- each direction has goal, value, KPIs, risks, and 12-month initiatives
- current-project directions and new fast-project directions are separated
- methodological directions do not replace commercial accountability

## Operating Model Gate

Reviewer mode: `operating-model-review`

Approve only if:

- center authority and responsibility are aligned
- stage-gate process has inputs, outputs, gate criteria, roles, and KPIs
- RACI covers center, R&D, commercial, production, finance, legal/IP, business owner, and partners
- handoff to business owner is explicit

## Board Strategy Gate

Reviewer mode: `board-review`

Approve only if:

- the strategy is management-ready
- commercial effect logic is visible
- weak portfolio areas are not hidden
- scenarios, roadmap, KPIs, and risks are specific enough for decision-making
- missing data is not disguised as certainty

## Red Team Gate

Reviewer mode: `red-team-review`

Approve only if:

- top vulnerabilities are explicit
- unsupported EBITDA attribution is challenged
- lack of authority, data, production capacity, or customer validation is surfaced
- required management decisions are named
- final revision instructions are actionable
