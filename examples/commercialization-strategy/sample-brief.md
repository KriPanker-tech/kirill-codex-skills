# Sample Brief

## Task

Build a commercialization strategy for a corporate R&D commercialization center.

## Decision To Support

The current R&D portfolio contains long and capital-intensive projects. The strategy should show why the center needs to add faster commercial projects, especially in small-tonnage chemistry, to create nearer-term business effects.

## Target Role Of The Center

The center should act as a methodology owner, creator of business concepts and business cases, and in selected cases an implementation driver.

## Scope

All projects in the available portfolio should be considered.

## Candor Level

The strategy may state weaknesses in projects directly when evidence supports the claim.

## External Sources

External market data and western company practices may be used, but they must be marked separately from document facts.

## Known Constraints

- Do not invent EBITDA, EMV, market size, or project readiness data.
- Mark missing data as `data insufficient`.
- Separate document facts, external benchmarks, expert hypotheses, and missing data.
