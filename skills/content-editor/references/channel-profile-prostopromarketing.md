# Простопромаркетинг Channel Overlay

Use this file only as a second layer on top of `content-editor`.

It is not a standalone writing skill.

Its role is to calibrate content specifically for Telegram channel `Простопромаркетинг`.

## Channel Intent

This channel is about marketing as diagnosis and architecture.

It exists to help readers see:

- where marketing systems actually break
- why demand does not become a controllable system
- where value gets lost between product, message, sales, and decision-making
- which weak assumptions quietly burn money, leads, and trust

This channel is not:

- a career news feed
- a masterclass showcase
- a generic marketing tips stream
- a diary of entrepreneurship
- a hype channel about AI tools

## Reader Contract

The reader should expect:

- one sharp idea per post
- a visible mechanism beneath the surface
- respect for their time and intelligence
- practical orientation without noisy tactics
- a channel that improves how they see the system, not just what they do next

## Topic Gate

Topics are core if they help reveal:

- hidden mistakes in value articulation
- weak demand generation logic
- blurred ICP and segmentation problems
- disconnects between marketing and sales
- economic losses caused by poor marketing structure
- content as a function inside demand systems
- AI as an amplifier of diagnosis and decision quality

Topics are secondary if they are only:

- market gossip
- job news
- platform news
- trend commentary with no structural takeaway
- personal updates without transferable insight

If the post does not clarify where the marketing system is broken, it is probably not core channel material.

## Format Gate

Preferred channel formats:

- diagnostic mistake
- hidden cause
- case without magic
- market anti-pattern
- practical frame
- false choice breakdown
- internal working scene, if it proves a larger point

Avoid publishing pieces that are mostly:

- broad opinion
- generic inspiration
- abstract education with no operating consequence
- sales-first promo disguised as content

## Tone Calibration

Compared with base `Telegram Mode`, this channel should be:

- slightly denser in meaning
- sharper in diagnosis
- more explicit about hidden mechanics
- less interested in entertaining movement for its own sake
- more alive in rhythm than a purely thesis-driven editorial note

Tone target:

- calm
- exact
- adult
- direct
- non-hysterical
- non-performative

Allowed:

- short hard conclusions when they are earned
- lived language if it helps the thought land
- clear criticism of weak logic
- a more flowing paragraph when it carries the reasoning better than a sequence of chopped lines
- small turns of thought that make the voice feel human rather than mechanically structured
- compact natural paragraphs that read like a person writing in Telegram, not like an outline broken into lines

Not allowed:

- infobiz urgency
- loud certainty theater
- career-media tone as the main voice
- casual everyday storytelling without analytical payoff
- over-chopped text where every line tries to sound like a hard takeaway
- editorial rigidity that removes natural movement from the prose
- paragraphing that looks like a deck of тезисы instead of a real post

## Anti-Drift Rules

Do not drift into:

- `Берегулина mode`: too much career-market framing, event/news feed logic, polished distance
- `Кот mode`: too much overt promotion, masterclass energy, sales pressure, formulaic promise language
- `Молянов mode`: too much business diary texture, loose topic spread, бытовая conversational drift

Benchmark traits may be borrowed only at the level of craft:

- from Берегулина: calm authority and mature framing
- from Кот: practical structure and fast usefulness
- from Молянов: human reality and working texture

But the center of gravity must remain:

- diagnosis
- hidden mechanics
- architecture of growth
- causal marketing logic

Rhythm rule for this channel:

- keep the text readable and alive
- do not turn every paragraph into a slogan
- use short lines as accents, not as the entire texture of the post
- if the draft sounds too "correct", rewrite toward a more natural speaking rhythm
- if several adjacent lines belong to one idea, keep them in one paragraph
- default to natural paragraph blocks; break the line only when you want emphasis, contrast, or a visible pivot

## Ending Rules

Good endings for this channel:

- a hard conclusion
- a reframed question
- a bridge to the next diagnostic point
- a soft invitation to discuss a similar situation

Weak endings for this channel:

- motivational uplift
- moral lesson with no mechanism
- pushy CTA
- empty summary of what was just said

## Final Channel Check

Before finalizing any `Простопромаркетинг` post, verify:

1. Is there one clear diagnostic spine?
2. Is the hidden mechanism visible?
3. Is the text channel-specific, or could it appear in any generic marketing feed?
4. Does it strengthen the channel's axis: marketing as diagnosis and architecture?
5. Does the post leave the reader with a better way to see the problem?
6. Does it sound alive, or has the editing made it too clipped and overly thesis-like?
7. Do the paragraph breaks feel natural, or has the text been chopped into lines the way people do not actually write?

If not, rewrite.
