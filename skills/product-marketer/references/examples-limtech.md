# Example: Limtech

Use `Limtech - лидогенерация и позиционирование` as a reference for:

- narrowing focus to a few priority products
- connecting ICP, pain, message, CTA, objections, and disqualifiers
- thinking about mini-GTM per product
- tying site, outbound, telemarketing, closers, and CRM into one chain

Key lesson:

Do not start from channels alone. Start from product, segment, pain, and commercial logic.
