# Commercialization Strategy Review Criteria

## Common Criteria

Check every stage for:

- unsupported facts or invented data
- unclear separation of facts, benchmarks, hypotheses, and missing data
- weak connection to commercial effect
- vague management language
- missing handoff to the next stage
- hidden blockers

## Portfolio Review

Check:

- all identifiable projects are included
- project fields are normalized
- missing fields are visible
- risks are categorized
- business model and effect horizon are not invented

## Gap Review

Check:

- strategic gap is evidenced or marked as hypothesis
- timing and readiness are clear
- R&D and commercial readiness are separated
- small-tonnage chemistry is not treated as a magic answer

## Directions Review

Check:

- directions are specific enough to manage
- current portfolio and new fast commercial contour are separated
- KPI logic is practical
- 12-month initiatives are real actions

## Operating Model Review

Check:

- center has authority where it has accountability
- RACI is not decorative
- stage gates have criteria
- handoff to business owners is explicit

## Board Review

Check:

- the document is decision-ready
- scenarios and risks are usable by management
- EBITDA / EMV attribution is defensible
- weak portfolio areas are named directly

## Red Team Review

Check:

- top vulnerabilities are explicit
- optimistic assumptions are challenged
- missing market and customer validation is named
- required management decisions are separated from analysis
