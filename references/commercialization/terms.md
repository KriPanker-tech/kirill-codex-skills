# Commercialization Terms

## Commercialization Center

An internal function that turns technology developments and market opportunities into business concepts, business cases, pilots, first implementations, and commercial effects.

## Business Case Factory

A repeatable capability for converting market opportunities and technology ideas into decision-ready business cases.

## Market-back R&D

R&D logic that starts from a market problem, customer need, or commercial opportunity instead of starting only from an existing technology.

## Fast Commercial Contour

A portfolio lane for faster commercial projects, especially small-tonnage chemistry opportunities where demand, production, and business model can be tested before major investment.

## Stage Gate

A decision point between workflow stages or project stages where continuation depends on evidence, risk, readiness, and ownership.

## Smart Pause

A managed pause for a project that should not continue in its current form until missing evidence, ownership, or economics are resolved.
